    A little bit about me

    Hi there, my name is Julia Grocholl. I am currently a Junior at the University of Maine and am studying finance and mathematics.
    I am originally from Brunswick, ME and love going back there to visit my family, dog, and two cats.
    I am a member of Pi Beta Phi here at UMaine. I joined as a sophomore and am so glad I did. It has given me the opportunity to be much more invloved and has lead me to lifelong friendships.
    In my free time I love to play around with watercolors and pastels. Although it is not part of my major, I enjoy being creative and keeping art a big part of my life.
    A few things that really make me happy are the beach, apple picking, animals, Christmas, cooking healthy meals, yoga, and art.
    I hope you learned something about me!
