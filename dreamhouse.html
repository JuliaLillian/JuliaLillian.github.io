    My Dream House

    Exterior

    Living Room

    I want my living room to have a farmhouse vibe. The furniture would be neutral and cozy with splashes of color on the pillows and decorations.

    Kitchen

    I like the look of the farmhouse sink and butcher block countertops. I also like open white cabinets and an industrial stove.

    Master Bedroom

    I want my bedroom to be simple with cozy bedding. I like the idea of neutral bedding and an area rug underneath.

    Playroom

    I like whites in the playroom with a really colorful rug and maybe even a colorful accent wall. A kitchen playset is a must along with an art station.

    Bathroom

    I really like a patterned black and white tile floor in the bathroom. I also like the wooden/rustic look and would like a seperate tub near a window.
